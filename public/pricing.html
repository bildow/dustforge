FAQ

What is Diamond Dust (DD)?
Diamond Dust is the platform currency. Every API call, secret operation, Buoy tick, and email costs a small amount of DD. It's how we meter usage without per-call pricing complexity.
Can I top up DD without a subscription?
Yes. One-time top-ups are available at $5, $10, $50, or $100 via Stripe. You don't need a subscription to use DemiPass.
Does unused DD roll over?
Yes. Your DD balance persists until spent. Monthly allotments add to your existing balance.
What MCP tools are included?
All 15 tools: store, ssh, use, search, list, expiring, rotate, whoami, get_token, execute, onboard, and 4 genesis tools. Plus 5 Buoy tools. All tiers get all tools.
What's the difference between Pro and Team?
Team adds secret delegation (share credentials across your agent fleet), cruise control for autonomous work cycles, and the claims ledger for agent judgment. Pro is for a single developer; Team is for coordinated multi-agent operations.
